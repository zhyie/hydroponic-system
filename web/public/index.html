What is Hydroponics?

The Hydroponic system is a new alternative cultivation technology that applies nutrients solution without the soil substrates, but with the presence of artificial supporting medium, offering the ability to reuse water and nutrients, ease of environmental variability, control, continuous production yield, and successive prevention of soil-borne disease and pests.

However, even experienced farmers who have adopted hydroponic systems for years, these farmers still face persistent problems such as high-water temperature, imbalanced water pH, and manual fertigation.

Project Overview

This project aims to develop and implement a system that addresses the problems faced in hydroponic farming and to improve the efficiency of hydroponic systems through automation.
